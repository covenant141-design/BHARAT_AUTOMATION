Our Genesis

Established in 2023 in Jaipur, Rajasthan, Bharat Automations was founded with a singular vision: to remove human subjectivity from the driver evaluation process.

We recognized that manual RTO testing often leads to bottlenecks, grievances, and compromised road safety. By leveraging cutting-edge computer vision and military-grade sensor networks, we developed an automated system capable of evaluating complex driving maneuvers with absolute mathematical precision.

"Our mission is to deploy indigenous technology that ensures only competent drivers receive licenses, ultimately saving lives on Indian roads."

Why Choose Us

Zero Latency

High Throughput
